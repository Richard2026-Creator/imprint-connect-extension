## Name
Pinterest Board Downloader

## Short description (max 132 characters)
Download images from any Pinterest board as a ZIP. Scan the board you're viewing, pick the pins you want, and save them in one click.

## Detailed description
Pinterest Board Downloader lets you save the images from any Pinterest board to your computer as a single ZIP file.

How it works:
1. Open the Pinterest board you want in your browser (your own boards, including private ones, work too because it uses your logged-in session).
2. Click the extension icon and press "Scan board". The extension scrolls the board and collects every pin.
3. A grid of thumbnails appears. Select all of them, or click individual pins to choose just the ones you want.
4. Click "Download ZIP" and choose where to save. Done.

Features:
- Works on public and private boards (uses your existing Pinterest login — no separate sign-in)
- Select all pins or pick individual ones
- Saves full-size images where available
- Everything runs locally in your browser — no servers, no accounts, no tracking

Privacy:
This extension collects no personal data and sends nothing to any server. All processing happens in your browser. See the privacy policy for details.

Note: This is an independent tool and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Pinterest. Please respect content creators' rights and Pinterest's terms of service when downloading images.
