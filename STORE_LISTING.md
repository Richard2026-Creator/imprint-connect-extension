## Name
IMPRINT Connect

## Short description (max 132 characters)
Curate Pinterest inspiration for your IMPRINT library. Scan a board, select the images you want, and download them as a ZIP.

## Detailed description
IMPRINT Connect helps you curate interiors inspiration from Pinterest and save it to your own library as a single ZIP file.

How it works:
1. Open the Pinterest board you want in your browser (your own boards, including private ones, work too because it uses your logged-in session).
2. Click the IMPRINT Connect icon and press "Scan Board". The extension scrolls the board and collects every pin.
3. A refined two-column grid of images appears. Select all of them, or click individual images to choose just the ones you want.
4. Click "Download ZIP" and choose where to save. Done.

Features:
- Works on public and private boards (uses your existing Pinterest login — no separate sign-in)
- Curated two-column image grid designed for aesthetic review
- Select all images or pick individual ones
- Saves full-size images where available
- Everything runs locally in your browser — no servers, no accounts, no tracking

Privacy:
IMPRINT Connect collects no personal data and sends nothing to any server. All processing happens in your browser. See the privacy policy for details.

Note: This is an independent tool and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Pinterest. Please respect content creators' rights and Pinterest's terms of service when downloading images.
